1. What are the types of the following values?

[’a’, ’b’, ’c’]
(’a’, ’b’, ’c’)
[(False, ’O’), (True, ’1’)]
([False, True ], [’0’, ’1’])
[tail, init, reverse ]

Answers: 

[’a’, ’b’, ’c’] 				:: [Char] 				//List of characters
(’a’, ’b’, ’c’)					:: (Char, Char, Char)	//Tuple
[(False, ’O’), (True, ’1’)]		:: [(Bool, Char)]		//List of Tuple
([False, True ], [’0’, ’1’])	:: ([Bool], [Char])		//Tuple of List
[tail, init, reverse ]			:: [[a]->[a]]			//List of Functions
